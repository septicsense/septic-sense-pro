Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a team of environmental engineers and septic system specialists, SepticSense was created with a simple mission: to make reliable septic system maintenance accessible to every homeowner and RV owner.

Over the years, we've evolved from a small consultancy to a resource hub, providing digital tools, expert guides, and maintenance plans that prevent costly environmental damage and maintain healthy septic systems. We combine traditional septic expertise with modern technology to deliver unmatched value and peace of mind.
